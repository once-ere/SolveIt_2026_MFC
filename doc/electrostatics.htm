The Electric Field Due to a Static Charge Distribution

Instruction Summary:

  • control-left-click in the 'System Window' to create a charged particle of charge +1
  • right-click and select 'fieldlines' to toggle fieldline production
  • the electric field through the point under the mouse cursor is drawn
  • an interval of the intersection of an equipotential surface through the point under the mouse cursor with the x-y plane is shown
  • control-left-drag a charged particle to move it
  • to remove a charged particle, left-click it then press the 'Delete' key
  • left-double-click on a charged particle to edit its charge
  • hold the shift key down while moving the mouse to show the vector electric fields through the points under the mouse cursor »×
