Energy Eigenvalues E for an Exciton Problem

Approach: Time-independent Schrodinger Equation solved using a step function approximation to the coeffcients of the Sturm-Liouville equation
- d/dx [ p(x) du/dx ] + q(x) u = E r(x) u .
Let
pn be the value of p on the nth piece,
qn be the value of q on the nth piece,
rn be the value of r on the nth piece,
un be the value of u on the nth piece,
u'n = [ p(x) du/dx ] on the nth piece
Let tn = (E rn - qn) / pn
wn = sqrt( | tn | )
sin(wn t)/wn t > A
fn(t) = sinh(wn t)/wn t < -A
t ( 1 - tn t^2 / 3! + t^2n t^4 / 5!) |t| < A

u(x) = un f 'n (x - xn) + u'n fn (x - xn)/ pn
